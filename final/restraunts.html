Explore Chicago's Culinary Scene

Chicago boasts a vibrant food culture with a wide range of dining options. From classic deep-dish pizza joints to upscale steakhouses, there's something for every palate.

Discover the following types of restaurants:

  • Girl & The Goat: A trendy spot known for its inventive American cuisine and lively atmosphere.
  • Giordano's: Famous for its Chicago-style deep-dish pizza.
  • Sweetwater Tavern & Grille: Offers city views, craft beer, and a lively ambiance.
  • RPM STEAK: A modern steakhouse with an award-winning wine list.
