Explore Chicago's Culinary Scene

Chicago boasts a vibrant food culture with a wide range of dining options. From classic deep-dish pizza joints to upscale steakhouses, there's something for every palate.

Discover the following types of restaurants:

  • Girl & The Goat: A trendy spot known for its inventive American cuisine and lively atmosphere.
  • Giordano's: Famous for its Chicago-style deep-dish pizza.
  • Sweetwater Tavern & Grille: Offers city views, craft beer, and a lively ambiance.
  • RPM STEAK: A modern steakhouse with an award-winning wine list.
  • Lou Malnati's Pizzeria: A Chicago classic known for its deep-dish pizza and delicious toppings.
  • Alinea: Award-winning fine dining offering a unique and innovative tasting experience.
  • Portillo's Hot Dogs: A Chicago staple known for its classic Chicago-style hot dogs and Italian beef sandwiches.
  • The Purple Pig: A vibrant eatery specializing in Mediterranean-inspired small plates and a diverse wine selection.
  • Gino's East: Famous for its deep-dish pizza, Gino's East is a Chicago institution with a rich pizza-making history.
  • Xoco: A casual Mexican eatery by Chef Rick Bayless, offering delectable street food-inspired dishes and artisanal chocolate.
